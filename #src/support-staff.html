For all roles, references will be taken up and Identity and DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks will be carried out. For further information please Contact Us.

Elmbridge Mencap has a board of Trustees who have overall legal responsibility for the charity. Trustees form the Executive Committee who meet once a month (usually Mondays) to conduct the business of the charity. This will include ensuring that the charity remains within the law, that money is spent wisely and for the benefit of the charity and making decisions in accordance with the charity's aims and objectives.

  • Volunteer oppourtunity

    Trustee member

    Are you interested in contributing to making a difference? Have you a few hours to spare and skills you feel could be useful? You may be just the person we are looking for!

    Due to retirements of longstanding trustees, we are looking for at least two trustees with a business background, who could bring a variety of skills and experience to supplement and complement our current board of Trustees. You may be required to take the lead on managing a specific project or assisting with a fundraising campaign or researching and drafting grant applications. Building a network of contacts within the community will also be important.

    You will need to have access to a computer and an email address. Meetings take place once a month at Burview Hall based in Walton on Thames, Surrey, however other areas of work e.g., policy drafting/amending, etc could be carried out at home. All Trustees are expected to help with fundraising activities such as the annual Christmas Fair which take place at the hall and to attend relevant training and meetings/seminars. You will also be required to be a member of one or more of our sub committees covering such matters as finance, fundraising, communications. These meet as and when the need arises. In addition, you may be asked to attend meetings with outside bodies to represent the charity.

  • Staff oppourtunity

    Day Service Manager and Day Service Support Assistant

    A Day Service Manager and Day Service Support Assistant required for our brand-new Day Service opening in the Autumn. This will be initially for three days a week but may increase if demand is there.

    Do you have previous experience of working with adults with learning disabilities and providing a range of activities tailored to individual needs? Would you like to be in at the start of something new and be able to shape the direction the service takes?

    Then we would love to hear from you!

    Competitive salary offered:

    £20 per hour for the Day Service Manager, £15 per hour for the Day Service Support Assistant

    For further details and an application pack please contact the Facilities Manager on 01932 221304 or elmbridgemencap@btconnect.com

    CLOSING DATE FOR RECEIPT OF APPLICATIONS: SUNDAY 21ST AUGUST 2022.

  • Volunteer oppourtunity

    Handy person

    To attend the hall on a Thursday morning between 9pm and 12pm to undertake minor repairs of equipment as required such as: changing light bulbs; painting and decorating; routine checking of fire alarm; arranging for servicing of equipment; snow clearance; drain clearance and checking; replenish paper towels, soap and catering items; identify low stocks; meter reading; light grounds maintenance. Please note: Could do other days of the week if required or as available. Useful to have a trade background such as plumbing, joinery, plastering etc. More important is to have all round practical skills and willingness to lend a hand. Would suit an active retired person. Need to live locally as useful to be available quickly if needed for emergency repairs etc.

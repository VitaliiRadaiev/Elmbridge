Trustees

Elmbridge Mencap has a board of Trustees who have overall legal responsibility for the charity. Trustees form the executive committee who meet once a month to conduct the business of the charity – this will include ensuring that the charity remains within the law in conducting its business, that money is spent wisely and for the benefit of the charity and making decisions in accordance with the charity’s aims and objectives.
